Current Exhibition

Ephemeral Echoes No. 4

by Elena Rostova

Medium

Oil on Belgian Linen

Dimensions

120 x 150 cm

Year

2023

Location

North Wing, Gallery A

Rostova’s fourth installment in the Ephemeral Echoes series explores the silent dialogue between geological permanence and the fleeting nature of light. The artist utilizes a restricted palette of earth tones to ground the viewer, while the gold leaf elements act as captures for ambient light, shifting throughout the day.

This piece was inspired by the rugged coastlines of the Outer Hebrides, specifically the interaction between monolithic stones and the morning mist.

Contemporary Abstract Earth Tones

The Artist

Elena Rostova (b. 1982) is a multidisciplinary artist based in Saint Petersburg. Her work is characterized by an obsession with texture and the physical properties of paint as it interacts with raw canvas.
